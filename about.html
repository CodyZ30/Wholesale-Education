Our Story

2020 - The Beginning

Founded by a team of successful entrepreneurs who recognized the lack of comprehensive wholesale business education. We started with a simple goal: make wholesale business knowledge accessible to everyone.

2021 - First Milestone

Launched our first comprehensive guide series and reached 10,000 learners. We expanded our content to cover major marketplaces and supplier networks.

2022 - Global Expansion

Expanded internationally, adding support for multiple languages and regional marketplaces. Our community grew to 25,000 active learners across 50+ countries.

2023 - Platform Evolution

Launched our comprehensive platform with tools, calculators, and interactive learning paths. Introduced our supplier directory and marketplace guides.

2024 - AI Integration

Integrated AI-powered tools for market analysis, supplier matching, and personalized learning recommendations. Reached 50,000+ active learners.

2025 - The Future

Continuing to innovate with advanced automation tools, expanded marketplace coverage, and deeper industry partnerships. Our vision: 1 million successful entrepreneurs by 2030.
